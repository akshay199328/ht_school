School Vision

The School’s Vision is to provide a “Better Tomorrow” for our children by giving them better education, which will fully equip them to face challenges of life in the field of Academics, Sports, Arts and Cultural activities with vocational and professional competence.

About Us

Air Force Golden Jubilee Institute is an integrated school where children of special needs are studying along with the mainstream students. This integrated concept serves the purpose of inculcating values of empathy, compassion, harmony, comradeship among students. The School has been imparting education since the year 1986. At present, an integrated group of about 2179 students and 91 students with special needs receive education with multi disciplinary approach for their all round development.
